Volunteer Activities

Volunteer and Mentor One Summer (VAMOS)

This is a community organization that aimed to provide free higher education to middle-school students from low-income families around Chicago. We worked together with mentors from other universities to develop a 6-week STEAM curricular for about 30 students each summer, and we also instructed them. The program has provided classes on programming, mathematics, science, aerospace, etc.

MAXIMO NIVEL

I joined this Study Abroad organization to volunteer for a Construction and Renovation project in Peru, Cusco. I collaborated with diverse people for two weeks, where we helped to build a dog shelter and a children's clinic.

KINETIC

It is a youth empowering organization where immigrant high school students were involved in immigrant and refugee youth advocacy and civic engagement.
